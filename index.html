Our Programs

Koding Kids

For coders with no experience at all

The Koding Kids course is designed to educate young students in basic computer science principles. By introducing them to computer science early with the visual coding language Scratch, this course aims to develop a solid foundation in coding so they may be prepared to learn more advanced-level languages.

Koding Kids Pro

For those with a good foundation

Koding Kids Pro takes students through an introductory course in Python. Through step by step instruction, students will learn the fundamentals of this programming language and develop the necessary skills to design and execute their own projects. Recommended for middle-schoolers and above with no experience in Python.
